### [Section 2: Credit Risk - RAROC Pricing Models](./section2_credit_risk/README.md)
**Focus**: Risk-based pricing and RAROC calculation for corporate lending

**Key Topics**:
- Credit risk fundamentals (PD, EAD, LGD, Expected Loss)
- Risk-based pricing methodology
- RAROC (Risk-Adjusted Return on Capital) calculation
- Goal Seek optimization analysis
- Sensitivity analysis

**Case Study**: EnergiCore (Pty) Ltd - R100M loan for sustainable industrial complex

**Main Tasks**:
- Risk assessment of the project
- Build 120-month amortization schedule
- Calculate RAROC for base case (13.75% interest rate)
- Goal Seek: Find rate for 17% RAROC target
- Goal Seek: Find break-even rate (Economic Profit = 0)
- Sensitivity analysis: PD vs RAROC (10 scenarios)
- Video presentation (5 minutes maximum)

**Deliverables**:
- Excel workbook with all models and analysis
- PowerPoint presentation
- Video recording

---

### [Section 3: ESG Analysis - Sustainability Comparison](./section3_esg/README.md)
**Focus**: Evaluating Environmental, Social, and Governance initiatives in banking

**Key Topics**:
- ESG framework and importance in banking
- Environmental sustainability metrics
- Social responsibility and diversity
- Governance frameworks and risk management
- ESG-related financial metrics (RAROC)

**Companies Analyzed**:
1. Green Horizon Bank - Net-zero by 2030, strong diversity
2. Blue Earth Finance - 50% carbon reduction by 2035, advanced cybersecurity

**Main Tasks**:
- Compare environmental initiatives and performance
- Analyze social responsibility programs
- Evaluate governance frameworks
- Rank overall ESG performance
- Provide improvement recommendations
- Calculate RAROC for both companies
- Explain RAROC drivers
- Identify long-term ESG benefits (minimum 3)
- Strategic recommendation on carbon financing
- Video presentation (5 minutes maximum)

**Deliverables**:
- PowerPoint presentation with comprehensive analysis
- Video recording
