## ğŸ“ Key Concepts

### Operational Risk (Basel)
Risk of loss from failed internal processes, people, systems, or external events.

### Credit Risk
Risk that borrower fails to meet loan obligations.

### RAROC
Risk-Adjusted Return on Capital - measures profitability relative to risk.

**Benchmark**: >15% is good for banks

### Hurdle Rate
Minimum acceptable return; typical: 15% for banks.

### ESG
Environmental, Social, Governance - framework for sustainability assessment.
